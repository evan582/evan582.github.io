Scrambled Eggs

Here is a recipe for Scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoons of butter
  • 2 Tablespoons of cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. one butter is melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat.
